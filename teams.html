Founder & Creative Director Arun Mohan

Born and raised in Arunachal Pradesh, this grounded yet adventurous Keralite moved to Mumbai in 2008 to pursue his creative ambitions. His journey began with wildlife photography, where he experienced and embraced both its challenges and rewards. Over time, his evolving vision led him to transition into Fashion and Commercial photography, which soon became his full-time profession. With over 14 years of industry experience, he specializes in both Photography and Cinematography. His core areas of expertise include Fashion & Commercials, Corporate photography, Food & Product imagery, and Architectural photography.

Founder & Director of Operations Aneesh Babu

Calm, composed, & highly professional, Aneesh Babu is a key pillar in the success of Camera & Wayfarer. A proactive leader by nature, he has played a crucial role in strengthening the company’s foundation & driving its growth. With a strong background in Finance, he brings deep expertise to his role, overseeing Finance & Operations with precision & strategic insight. His experience with reputed financial institutions, combined with a genuine passion for the creative industry, enables him to bridge structure with innovation. Known for balancing professional excellence with meaningful social impact, Aneesh exemplifies leadership that inspires trust, stability, & sustainable growth.
